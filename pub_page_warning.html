Q-sort Publication Images

This page is for making publication-ready images of sample Q-sorts from your project. Once you have positioned all of the statements, press and continue to hold the Sleep/Wake button on your iPad. Then press and release the Home button. If successful, you should hear the camera shutter sound. An image of the Q-sort is now in your Photos application.

To exit the Publication Image Q-sorter page, tap the the bottom right corner of the screen.
